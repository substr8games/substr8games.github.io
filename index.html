About SUBSTR8.Games

SUBSTR8.Games is an independent software and games studio owned by Giz, a full-stack, independent kernel architect and software engineer from Los Angeles, CA.

SUBSTR8.Games exercises its right to harshly critique and outright reject the assumptions and narratives of the modern software industry and entrenched legacy and legacy-style tech giants like Microsoft, Oracle, Meta, and X/SpaceX, as well as the narratives driven by lowest-common-denominator outlets and communities like Reddit and Discord, as well as social media/attention economy apparatuses like Facebook, Instagram, Youtube, X, Tiktok. 

SUBSTR8.Games' mandate is to operate within the bounds of the law and without any regard whatsoever for what is perceived as normal (boring), nice (placating), friendly (deceptive), approachable (over-simplified), or otherwise deemed appropriate by those whose delicate sensibilities we offend.

SUBSTR8.Games is not your nana's future-tech company.

No, we will not apologize.

Yes, we will deliver.

To borrow the words of one of the great cinematic archetypes of all time: we will carry you kicking and screaming, and in the end, you will thank us.

End transmission.
